Welcome to RL

Here every moment matters

At RL our purpose is to ensure that the people we care for are at the centre of the health and social care services we provide. our desire is to make everyone feel empowered, safe and valued at our establishment.

RL Care

A national provider of care and support with a diverse range of services. The people we support have complex needs, they may have autism, learning disabilities, mental health issues or need rehab in a specialist nursing service. We believe that with the right health and social care support everyone can achieve greater independence and lead fulfilling lives. Each person we care for is at the heart of the services we provide.

RL Values

Caring for individuals with complex needs, including learning disabilities and autism, to live life to the full. We work with each person we care and support, their family members and network, to deliver high quality meaningful care. Enabling each person we support to live a full and rich life. Helping them access the opportunities and activities open to everyone in their community.
